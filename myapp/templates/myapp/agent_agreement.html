Student Recruitment Agreement
MIATA – Medical Imaging Training Academy

This Agreement is made between:

MIATA – Medical Imaging Training Academy Address: Level 3, 51 Queen St, Melbourne Victoria 3000
(Hereinafter referred to as "MIATA")
Agent / Agency (Hereinafter referred to as "Agent")
Collectively referred to as "the Parties".

1. Appointment

1.1 MIATA appoints the Agent on a non-exclusive, non-transferable basis to promote and recruit eligible students for MIATA's Professional / Post Graduate Diploma in Medical Imaging program.

1.2 This Agreement does not create a partnership, joint venture, employment, franchise, or agency relationship beyond student referral activities.

1.3 The Agent has no authority to bind MIATA contractually or financially.

2. Program Description

Program Duration: 1 Year

  • 6 Months Online Theory
  • 3 Months Practical Hands-on Training
  • 6 Months Internship (with stipend)

Eligibility includes MBBS (including foreign medical graduates), BHMS, BAMS, BNYS, and other approved health science qualifications.

3. Strict Prohibition on Fee Collection

⚠️ The Agent shall NOT collect any fees from students on behalf of MIATA.

3.1 The Agent shall not collect, receive, demand, or accept any monetary payment from prospective students on behalf of MIATA.

3.2 The Agent shall not charge service fees, admission fees, processing fees, or "guarantee fees" in MIATA's name.

3.3 All tuition fees must be paid directly to MIATA's official bank account.

3.4 Any breach of this clause shall result in:

  • Immediate termination
  • Forfeiture of unpaid commission
  • Legal action for misrepresentation and damages

4. Commission Structure

Commission shall be calculated on tuition fees actually received by MIATA, excluding taxes and refunds.

Enrolled StudentsCommission Rate
First 5–10 students10%
11–30 students12%
31–50 students15%
Above 50 studentsSeparate written agreement

Commission is payable only after: (a) Student enrollment is confirmed, (b) Tuition fees are received by MIATA in full, (c) Cooling-off period (if applicable) has expired.

If a student withdraws or receives a refund, the corresponding commission shall not be payable or shall be clawed back.

5. Industry Standard Commission Disclosure

The Parties acknowledge that Australian higher education institutions commonly offer commission structures ranging between 10% to 20% of tuition fees, depending on volume and performance.

6. Agent Obligations

The Agent shall:

  • 6.1 Provide accurate, current, and written information approved by MIATA.
  • 6.2 Avoid false representations regarding immigration outcomes, income guarantees, or employment assurances.
  • 6.3 Comply with Australian Consumer Law and fair-trading standards.
  • 6.4 Maintain proper records of student communications.
  • 6.5 Protect student data in accordance with Australian privacy principles.

The Agent shall indemnify MIATA against claims arising from misrepresentation, negligence, or unlawful conduct.

7. Compliance with Australian Law

7.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and interpreted in accordance with the laws of the Commonwealth of Australia.

7.2 Any dispute shall first be resolved through good-faith negotiation.

7.3 If unresolved, disputes shall be submitted to arbitration in Australia.

7.4 Courts of Australia shall have exclusive jurisdiction.

8. Intellectual Property

8.1 MIATA retains full ownership of all course materials, branding, trademarks, curriculum, and marketing content.

8.2 The Agent may use MIATA branding strictly for approved recruitment activities only during the term of this Agreement.

9. Confidentiality

All commercial information, fee structures, student data, and training materials are confidential. This obligation survives termination for a period of five (5) years.

10. Term and Termination

10.1 Term: 1 year from signing date.

10.2 Either Party may terminate with 30 days written notice.

10.3 Immediate termination applies in case of:

  • Fee collection violation
  • Fraud or misrepresentation
  • Reputational damage
  • Breach of confidentiality

Upon termination, the Agent must cease using MIATA branding immediately.

11. Indemnity

The Agent agrees to indemnify and hold harmless MIATA from any losses, claims, penalties, regulatory fines, or reputational damage arising from the Agent's misconduct or breach.

12. Non-Solicitation

The Agent shall not independently offer competing medical imaging programs using MIATA's materials or confidential information during the term and for 12 months thereafter.

13. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties and supersedes prior discussions. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both Parties.
